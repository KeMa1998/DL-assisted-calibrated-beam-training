Added simulation files in major revision of IEEE Transactions on Communications.
(1) ASE: average spectral efficiency.
(2) Baseline3: added baseline 3 [1].
(3) Beam_number: simulation results under different output beam numbers.
(4) Model: investigation on model complexities and training parameters.
[1] S. Chiu, N. Ronquillo, and T. Javidi, “Active learning and CSI acquisition for mmWave initial alignment,” IEEE J. Sel. Areas Commun., vol. 37, no. 11, pp. 2474–2489, Nov. 2019.
